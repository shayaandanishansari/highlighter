The Idea



##### **Good ideas are slept on**

##### **Great work misses it's audience**

##### **Positive Change is delayed and delayed and ...**



**Being a young person seeing the world around you with it's beauty and ugliness I have always wanted to do something about the ugliness. Broken systems, bad food, poor infrastructure, inefficient use of resources. Don't get me wrong there's plenty of good out there, plenty of blue skies and I love blue skies.**



**And that's what I want to fight for, the good and the better. And there are so many ways I can, we can. So many ideas that can be enacted on, so much good work that can be done to make things better.**



**But... I have to be honest now. My incentive structure, and of many of those around me has not made sense for us to work on these "ideas" and do the "necessary work" to make them real. To get by in the world you need not change anything much at all, and this is the path of least resistance and so the water flows a lot in this direction.**



**Highlighter aims to change that. A platform where ideas are shared, and projects started. Write an article on something that's amazing and worth protecting and being happy about, or something broken that someone else might have a fix for.**



**And most importantly "post a project", and collaborators find you! That's how the incentive structure to create a positive change can really happen, and I hope very much that it does. 

If this remotely seems like something you'd find interesting please follow the journey!**
